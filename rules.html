LifestyleRP Rules

In-Server Laws

Built on U.S. law, with our own twists:

  1. Weed's legal with a "weed card"—max 6 plants.
  2. Speed limits: 35 in city, 55 outside. Simple.
  3. Pistols OK in public. Shotguns? Only on your turf. Autos are banned everywhere. Cops ask you to stow it first (just a citation), but refuse and it's cuffs time.
  4. No street parking in town unless it's got meters.

Rules

  1. Common sense is king.
  2. Respect everyone—no trash talk or hate speech, ever.
  3. No FailRP. Stick to real-life vibes. No serial killer sprees—keep it fun for all. Also, no phone fiddling in cuffs.
  4. No metagaming. Use only what your character knows. Head tags? OOC only, not RP.
  5. Follow the Renewed Life Rule (RLR)—details below.
  6. Props are for RP, not parkour or pushing.
  7. No hacking. Caught? Permanent IP ban.
  8. Ban evasion = IP ban + all accounts torched.
  9. Drive like you mean it. Pursuits can get wild, but no fail-driving otherwise.
  10. Obey cops unless you're packing a warrant or contraband—RP smart.
  11. No powergaming. Forcing RP (like kidnapping without OOC OK) is out. Robberies? Fair game.
  12. No cop baiting for kicks. This is RP, not GTA.
  13. No combat logging—stick around for the action.

Basing & Raiding

  1. Raid whoever, whenever—up to 10 players. Grab loot, dip in 10 mins max. No camping or claiming. Wait 45 mins to hit the same spot. If raided, stay out 'til it's over (10 mins). Max 10 raiders, org required, lockpick unless unlocked. No store basing/raiding.
  2. Mugging or kidnapping? OOC consent or bust.
  3. No RDM. Kill only to save yourself or others.
  4. Barricades must look legit.
  5. Orgs cap at 10—break it, and it's gone.

Rackets

No bullet-soaked heroics to defend—just RP it real. Names? Keep 'em tame, no celebs or politics. Admins might rename you if it's whack.

Group-Specific Rules

Citizen

  1. Cuffed? Follow orders unless your crew's springing you. No solo car escapes.
  2. Cops raiding? Fight back, but don't return 'til they're out.

Police

  1. Traffic stops: Lights, sirens, maybe a "Pull over!" yell.
  2. Need probable cause—speeding, crimes, reports. Ask if unsure.
  3. Radio up (T or /radio)—stops, calls, backup, all of it.
  4. Warrants need proof—like !lookup or a witness.
  5. Cuffed? They're yours, but orgs might crash the party.
  6. Raids: Seize illegals, arrest the target (or anyone dirty). Downed? Return only if called.
  7. No dirty cops. Shotgun out? Tell 'em to stow it first—arrest only if they defy.

Medic / Firefighter

  1. Not cops—stay out of the fray. Stage safe, move in post-clear.
  2. Chill anywhere between calls—RP it up.

RLR (Renewed Life Rule)

You don't die—you respawn as you. Here's the deal:

  1. 5-min recovery post-hospital before heading back.
  2. Raid/event downed (non-cop)? Wait 'til it's done + recovery.
  3. No revenge—new beef needs new reasons.

Cops can nab you at the hospital. Wanted? Jail after recovery.
